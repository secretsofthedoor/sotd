The History Of SOTD

31st August 2024

At the time of writing this, 55 months have passed since SOTD was created.

But here's how it started

On the 13th of January 2020, at approximately 11:15am, two people discovered a door.

Luckily, they were in possession of a tool, which would later become "the door tool".

They stayed at the door for around 10 minutes.

When they left the door, the door was no longer an ordinary door. The door had secrets.

Shortly afterwards, they mentioned "The Secrets Of The Door" to many people, without saying what it was

This led to many people being curious about what these "Secrets Of The Door" were

So they decided to create SOTD (Secrets Of The Door).

They told everybody that to join SOTD they must find out the Secrets Of The Door

They declared that to find out the Secrets Of The Door, they must "use the door tool on the door"

On the 15th of January 2020, they made the SOTD website (this website).

They then published clues on the website, which are still available to read.

By then, a lot of people were attempting to find out the door's secrets.

On the 31st of January 2020, 2 people working together used the door tool on the door and found out the Secrets Of The Door.

These people, who are codenamed "Orie" and "EBT" became members of SOTD.

In March 2020, coronavirus meant that nobody was able to be in the premises which the door is on

In the next few months after that, SOTD's popularity decreased.

However, the four members of SOTD were still regularly discussing it.

There was talk of reinstating SOTD after covid, but this never happened.

SOTD stopped in June 2020.

The clues are still available to read on the website.

The door still stands today, holding great secrets.

And for anybody who did not complete SOTD before it stopped, the Secrets Of The Door will always remain a mystery.
